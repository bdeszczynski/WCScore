Rules

Points teams earn 3 points for a win and 1 point for a draw in every round they play.

In knockout rounds, a team that loses on penalties also earns 1 bonus point.

Standings sort by points, then goal difference, then total goals scored.

Goals For, Goals Against, and Goal Difference are shown as scoring tiebreakers.

Every selected team earns 3 bonus points for reaching the semi-finals and 7 bonus points for winning the World Cup.
